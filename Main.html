Company Background

Founded in 2018, FastGreen Cargo Forwarding has been delivering fast, reliable, and transparent logistics solutions to businesses across the Philippines. Known for efficient operations, accurate documentation, responsive customer support, and real-time updates, the company has earned the trust of its growing client base. As one of the early logistics partners of Aice in the Philippines, FastGreen has continually expanded its services and now operates as a corporation. Guided by its commitment to innovation, speed, and sustainability, FastGreen remains dedicated to providing logistics solutions that are faster and greener than ever.
